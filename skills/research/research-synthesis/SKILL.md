# Research Synthesis

## Overview

Synthesis is where research becomes value. It compresses evidence into patterns, tensions, and implications without erasing uncertainty.

## When to Use

- After interview rounds or mixed-method studies
- Combining desk research with primary findings
- Building insight decks and opportunity maps
- Aligning teams on “what we know” vs “what we guess”

## Core Practices

- Affinity-map or code evidence systematically
- Separate observations, insights, and recommendations
- Note strength of evidence and contradictory data
- Frame insights as tension or human truth + implication
- Prioritize by decision relevance, not only interestingness
- Keep an evidence trail back to sources

## Principles

- Synthesis is analysis, not highlight-reeling quotes
- Contradictions are findings, not annoyances to hide
- Recommendations should follow from insights, not precede them
- Clarity for decision-makers is a research deliverable

## Verification

- [ ] Themes are evidenced, not only asserted
- [ ] Confidence levels are honest
- [ ] Implications connect to stakeholder decisions
