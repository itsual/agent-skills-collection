# Product-Led Growth

## Overview

In PLG, the product is the primary vehicle for growth. Users experience value before (or with minimal) sales intervention, and expansion follows naturally from usage.

## When to Use

- Designing freemium, free trial, or self-serve motions
- Improving activation and time-to-value
- Building in-product upgrade and expansion paths
- Shifting from sales-led to product-led or hybrid models

## Core Levers

- Time-to-value and activation experience
- Value metric aligned with willingness to pay
- In-product education and aha moments
- Viral / network / collaboration loops
- Upgrade prompts tied to natural usage limits
- Instrumentation of the full funnel

## Principles

- Deliver real value before asking for commitment
- Make the path from free → paid obvious and fair
- Design for expansion, not only initial conversion
- Align packaging with how value accumulates in the product
- Use data to find friction in self-serve journeys

## Verification

- [ ] Users can reach value without human intervention
- [ ] Conversion and expansion paths are instrumented
- [ ] Growth loops are intentional, not accidental
