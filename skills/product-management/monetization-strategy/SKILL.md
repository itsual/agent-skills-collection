# Monetization Strategy

## Overview

Monetization is more than a price tag. It includes what you charge for, how you package value, how customers expand, and how the motion fits the market.

## When to Use

- Designing or redesigning revenue models
- Improving expansion and net revenue retention
- Aligning packaging with product-led or sales-led motions
- Evaluating usage-based, seat-based, tiered, or hybrid models

## Core Dimensions

- Revenue model (subscription, usage, transactional, hybrid)
- Value metric
- Packaging and tier architecture
- Free / trial strategy
- Expansion paths (seats, usage, modules, overages)
- Discounting and exception governance
- Billing and packaging operations

## Principles

- Align what you charge for with the value customers perceive
- Make upgrading feel like a natural next step, not a punishment
- Optimize for long-term customer value, not only initial conversion
- Test major monetization changes carefully

## Verification

- [ ] Value metric is intuitive to customers
- [ ] Expansion paths exist and are measurable
- [ ] Monetization supports, rather than fights, the product experience
