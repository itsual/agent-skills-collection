# Illustration in Product

## Overview

Product illustration can humanize and clarify, but it can also distract or delay. The best product illustration serves a job: orientation, emotional support, or explanation.

## When to Use

- Empty states and first-run moments
- Onboarding and educational moments
- Brand expression in product shells
- Simplifying abstract concepts

## Core Practices

- Define the job of the illustration before drawing
- Match style to product brand and tone
- Keep file weight and rendering performance in mind
- Provide meaningful alternatives when illustration carries information
- Avoid forcing illustration where UI clarity is sufficient

## Principles

- Illustration supports the message; it is not the message
- Consistency of style builds a cohesive world
- Cultural sensitivity and inclusivity matter in figurative work
- Test whether illustration helps or merely decorates

## Verification

- [ ] Illustration has a clear purpose on the screen
- [ ] Core task remains obvious
- [ ] Style aligns with the broader design system
