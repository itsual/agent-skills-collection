# Deferred Revenue Management

## Overview

Deferred revenue represents cash or billings received before revenue is earned. Managing the rollforward is central to subscription finance integrity.

## When to Use

- Subscription and prepaid billing models
- Billings vs revenue analysis
- Deferred revenue rollforwards for audit and board
- System design for revenue schedules

## Core Practices

- Maintain contract-level or cohort-level revenue schedules
- Produce deferred revenue rollforwards (begin + billings − revenue = end)
- Align billing terms with recognition systems
- Review lingering deferred balances for completeness
- Explain billings–revenue gaps in management reporting

## Principles

- Deferred revenue is a liability, not a vanity metric alone
- Growing deferred can be healthy (prepaid growth) or a recognition lag problem
- Systems must schedule recognition automatically where possible
- Manual schedules don’t scale

## Verification

- [ ] Rollforward reconciles to GL
- [ ] Recognition schedules match contracts
- [ ] Billings vs revenue is explainable
