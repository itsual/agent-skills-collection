# Working Capital

## Overview

Working capital is the cash embedded in day-to-day operations. Improving it is often faster and cheaper than raising external capital.

## When to Use

- Cash conversion cycle improvement
- AR aging and collections programs
- AP terms strategy
- Inventory optimization with operations

## Core Practices

- Measure DSO, DPO, and DIO (or equivalent)
- Segment AR by risk and size for collections focus
- Negotiate payment terms intentionally, not accidentally
- Align inventory policy with demand uncertainty
- Watch for improvements that merely shift pain to partners unsustainably

## Principles

- Sustainable working capital beats one-time squeezes
- Operations and finance must own this together
- Growth often worsens working capital before it helps
- Metrics without process change don’t free cash
