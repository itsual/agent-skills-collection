# Payroll Finance

## Overview

Payroll is often the largest expense. Finance ensures accurate cost recognition, timely remittances, and insight into workforce cost drivers.

## When to Use

- Payroll accrual and close processes
- Workforce cost analysis
- Multi-jurisdiction payroll oversight
- Bonus and commission accounting

## Core Practices

- Accrue wages, bonuses, commissions, and related taxes
- Reconcile payroll registers to GL
- Ensure tax and statutory remittances are on time
- Analyze headcount, cost per FTE, and contractor mix
- Coordinate with HR on equity comp accounting impacts

## Principles

- Payroll errors are high-visibility and high-trust issues
- Accruals prevent period distortion
- Total workforce cost includes more than salaries
- Segregation of duties applies strongly in payroll

## Verification

- [ ] Payroll to GL reconciliation is routine
- [ ] Accruals cover material variable pay
- [ ] Statutory obligations are calendared
