# Fundraising Finance

## Overview

Fundraising finance makes the company’s story numerically credible. Investors buy future outcomes backed by historical integrity and realistic plans.

## When to Use

- Equity seed to growth rounds
- Debt raises and venture debt
- Bridge financing
- Investor diligence response

## Core Practices

- Ensure historical financials are clean and reconcilable
- Build a forecast investors can diligence (drivers, not magic)
- Calculate runway and use of proceeds clearly
- Prepare data room: financials, cohort metrics, contracts, cap table
- Anticipate diligence questions on growth efficiency and unit economics

## Principles

- Credibility compounds; polish over truth backfires
- Runway assumes realistic burn, not best case only
- Inconsistencies across decks and models destroy trust
- Finance and founders must tell the same story

## Verification

- [ ] Historicals tie to source systems
- [ ] Forecast drivers are explicit
- [ ] Data room is complete for the stage of raise
