# ESG and Sustainability Finance

## Overview

ESG finance connects environmental, social, and governance factors to measurement, capital allocation, and disclosure. Rigor matters; greenwash destroys trust.

## When to Use

- Sustainability metric and report support
- ESG-linked loans or bonds
- Capex cases with climate or social impacts
- Investor ESG questionnaires

## Core Practices

- Define material ESG topics for the business
- Establish measurable KPIs with owners and data sources
- Apply same control mindset as financial reporting where disclosed
- Evaluate investments with both financial and impact lenses when claimed
- Align disclosures to relevant frameworks carefully

## Principles

- Materiality first — don’t measure everything
- Claims require evidence
- Finance’s role is integrity of numbers and capital trade-offs
- Regulatory expectations are rising; stay current with advisors

## Verification

- [ ] Material topics and metrics are defined
- [ ] Data quality matches disclosure ambition
- [ ] Financial implications of ESG commitments are modeled
