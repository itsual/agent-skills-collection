# At-Risk Playbooks

## Overview

At-risk playbooks turn health signals into consistent action. They ensure similar risks get similar quality of response — and that outcomes are learned from.

## When to Use

- Health score drops and risk flags
- Executive sponsor loss, usage collapse, or competitive threats
- Pre-renewal risk mitigation
- Creating standard operating plays for CSMs

## Core Practices

- Trigger criteria for each playbook (what puts an account in-play)
- Diagnostic checklist to identify root cause category
- Intervention steps by cause (adoption, value, support, commercial, political)
- Escalation paths and executive involvement rules
- Required documentation in CRM/CS tools
- Measure save outcomes and time-to-intervention

## Principles

- Speed of diagnosis beats generic check-in calls
- One playbook cannot cover all risk types — branch by cause
- Documented attempts enable coaching and pattern analysis
- Not every at-risk account is savable on acceptable terms

## Verification

- [ ] Plays have clear triggers and steps
- [ ] CRM hygiene captures cause and outcome
- [ ] Intervention SLA performance is reviewed
