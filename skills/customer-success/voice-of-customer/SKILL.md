# Voice of Customer (CS)

## Overview

CS sits on a continuous stream of customer truth. VoC systems turn anecdotes into structured insight that product and leadership can use — and close the loop back to customers.

## When to Use

- Structuring CSM insight capture
- Thematic analysis of risks, requests, and praise
- Feeding product roadmap prioritization
- Executive insight packs from the installed base

## Core Practices

- Capture insights in structured fields (not only free text noise)
- Tag themes: adoption blockers, feature gaps, competitive mentions, value wins
- Synthesize frequency × severity × segment
- Route to Product, Marketing, and leadership with clear owners
- Close the loop when customer input influences outcomes
- Protect confidential commercial details appropriately

## Principles

- Loudest customer ≠ most important pattern
- VoC without routing is a journal, not a system
- Closing the loop increases future signal quality
- CS insight complements, not replaces, formal research

- [ ] Capture process is lightweight enough that CSMs use it
