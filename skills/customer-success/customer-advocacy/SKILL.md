# Customer Advocacy

## Overview

Advocacy amplifies real success into trust for prospects and product learning for the company. It must be earned and never coerced.

## When to Use

- Reference and referral program design
- Case study and review programs
- Advisory boards and champion communities
- Matching advocates to sales opportunities

## Core Practices

- Identify advocates based on outcomes and willingness, not only ACV
- Make participation easy and rewarding (non-coercive)
- Maintain a living reference database with preferences and constraints
- Capture stories close to success moments
- Protect advocate relationships from over-asking
- Close the loop: share impact of their help

## Principles

- Unhappy customers should never be asked to advocate
- Quality of reference fit beats quantity
- Advocacy is a CS + Marketing partnership
- Respect confidentiality and approval processes

## Verification

- [ ] Advocate criteria and consent are clear
- [ ] Reference requests are tracked and not abusive of the same logos
- [ ] Stories are current and approved
