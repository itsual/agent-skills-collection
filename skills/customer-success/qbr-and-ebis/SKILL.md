# QBR and Executive Business Reviews

## Overview

QBRs/EBRs are structured conversations about outcomes, not product tours. Done well, they reinforce value, surface risk, and set joint priorities.

## When to Use

- Recurring strategic account reviews
- Pre-renewal executive alignment
- Expansion opportunity framing
- Relationship reset after turbulence

## Core Practices

- Lead with customer goals and outcomes achieved
- Show adoption and impact with their metrics where possible
- Address risks and open issues honestly
- Align on next-period priorities and mutual actions
- Tailor altitude: operational QBR vs executive EBR
- Follow up with written summary and owners

## Principles

- Customer’s time is the scarce resource — earn it
- No surprises for executives in the room
- Slides support dialogue; they are not the deliverable alone
- A QBR without next steps is a presentation, not a review

## Verification

- [ ] Agenda centered on outcomes and decisions
- [ ] Right stakeholders attended or were briefed
- [ ] Actions and owners captured after the meeting
