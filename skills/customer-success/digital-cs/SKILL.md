# Digital CS (Tech-Touch)

## Overview

Digital CS delivers success motions through product, automation, and pooled teams. It is how companies support many customers well without linear headcount growth.

## When to Use

- Low-ACV or high-volume segments
- Hybrid models (digital baseline + human surge)
- Lifecycle campaigns for adoption and renewal
- Scaling CS without proportional hiring

## Core Practices

- Design digital journeys for onboarding, adoption, risk, renewal
- Trigger outreach from behavioral signals, not only calendars
- Provide self-serve success resources in-product
- Use pooled CSMs/specialists for exceptions and escalations
- Measure engagement quality and outcome rates by journey
- Continuously test messaging and timing

## Principles

- Digital is a designed experience, not “no CS”
- Automation without relevance becomes spam
- Human escalation paths must remain obvious
- Segment ruthlessly — digital fit is not universal

## Verification

- [ ] Journeys map to segment outcomes
- [ ] Triggers are behavior-based where possible
- [ ] Escalation to human help is clear and measured
