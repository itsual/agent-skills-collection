# Customer Education

## Overview

Customer education scales success by teaching users to achieve outcomes independently. It complements high-touch CS and reduces repetitive support demand.

## When to Use

- Building a customer academy or learning center
- Role-based training paths (admin, end user, champion)
- Certification programs
- Reducing “how do I” support volume

## Core Practices

- Design learning paths around jobs and roles, not feature lists only
- Mix formats: short videos, docs, interactive, live workshops
- Keep content current with product releases
- Measure consumption, completion, and impact on adoption/support
- Enable champions to train others internally
- Localize where customer base requires it

## Principles

- Just-in-time beats just-in-case for many topics
- Education ROI shows up in adoption, retention, and support deflection
- Outdated content destroys trust faster than no content
- Champions multiply education reach

## Verification

- [ ] Paths exist for primary roles
- [ ] Content ownership and update process are clear
- [ ] Impact metrics are defined (not only pageviews)
