# Adoption Programs

## Overview

Adoption programs move customers from shallow use to meaningful use. They target specific behaviors tied to value realization, not vanity login counts alone.

## When to Use

- Low feature adoption after onboarding
- Launching new modules to the installed base
- Segment-wide adoption campaigns
- Recovering dormant or shallow-usage accounts

## Core Practices

- Define adoption as behaviors linked to outcomes
- Identify activation milestones per persona/use case
- Combine in-product guides, CS outreach, and education
- Prioritize accounts where adoption unlocks retention/expansion
- Measure adoption depth, not only binary “used once”
- Feed friction findings to Product

## Principles

- Adoption without value is theater
- One more training email is not a program
- Different personas need different adoption paths
- Product changes often beat heroic CSM intervention at scale

## Verification

- [ ] Target behaviors map to customer outcomes
- [ ] Program has measurable adoption goals
- [ ] Results are reviewed and iterated
