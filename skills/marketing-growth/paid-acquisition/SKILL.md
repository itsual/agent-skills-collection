# Paid Acquisition

## Overview

Paid acquisition buys attention. It works when creative, targeting, landing experience, and economics align — and when learning is systematic, not chaotic.

## When to Use

- Launching paid channels (SEM, paid social, etc.)
- Scaling what already works
- Improving CAC, ROAS, or payback period
- Testing new audiences or offers quickly

## Core Practices

- Define target CAC / payback before scaling spend
- Align creative and landing page to the same promise
- Structure campaigns for learning (not only delivery)
- Track full-funnel conversion, not only clicks
- Kill losers fast; scale winners carefully
- Watch for attribution traps and creative fatigue

## Principles

- Unit economics first — growth that loses money on every customer isn’t growth
- Creative is often the biggest lever
- Landing experience is part of the ad system
- Don’t scale noise

## Verification

- [ ] Success metrics and constraints are defined pre-spend
- [ ] Tracking supports optimization decisions
- [ ] Learn/kill/scale rules are explicit
