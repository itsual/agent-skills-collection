# Conversion Optimization

## Overview

Conversion optimization increases the percentage of people who complete a desired action. It’s a research and experimentation practice, not random button-color testing.

## When to Use

- Landing page and website optimization
- Signup, activation, or checkout funnels
- Pricing page and upgrade conversion
- Reducing friction in high-intent journeys

## Core Practices

- Diagnose with data + qualitative insight (session replay, interviews, heatmaps)
- Form hypotheses tied to user friction or motivation
- Prioritize tests by impact × confidence × ease
- Run experiments with adequate power and clean measurement
- Document wins, losses, and learnings

## Principles

- Understand why people don’t convert before changing UI
- One primary CTA and message per view in most cases
- Speed, clarity, and trust often beat cleverness
- Optimization without traffic or statistical discipline wastes time

## Verification

- [ ] Hypotheses are grounded in evidence
- [ ] Experiments have clear primary metrics
- [ ] Results (including negatives) are recorded
