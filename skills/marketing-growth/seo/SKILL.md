# SEO

## Overview

SEO connects people searching for solutions with your content and product. Sustainable SEO balances technical foundations, intent-matched content, and earned authority.

## When to Use

- Growing organic acquisition
- Launching or restructuring content hubs
- Diagnosing traffic declines
- Aligning product pages with search demand

## Core Pillars

- **Technical** — crawlability, speed, indexation, structured data
- **Content** — intent match, depth, clarity, freshness
- **Authority** — links, brand mentions, trust signals
- **Experience** — UX signals that support engagement and conversion

## Principles

- Search intent > keyword density
- Don’t build pages only for bots
- Cannibalization and thin content destroy efficiency
- SEO and content strategy should be one system
- Measure rankings, traffic quality, and conversions — not rankings alone

## Verification

- [ ] Priority pages are indexable and fast
- [ ] Content matches the intent of target queries
- [ ] Organic performance is tracked to business outcomes
