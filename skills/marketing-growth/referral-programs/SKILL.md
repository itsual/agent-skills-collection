# Referral Programs

## Overview

Referral programs formalize word-of-mouth. They work when the product is already loved and the incentive aligns with both referrer and referee.

## When to Use

- Products with strong NPS / advocacy potential
- PLG motions seeking organic acquisition leverage
- Launching or tuning double-sided referral rewards

## Core Practices

- Ensure product value is real before incentivizing shares
- Make sharing frictionless (links, codes, in-product prompts)
- Design incentives for both sides when possible
- Prevent fraud and self-referral abuse
- Track referral quality, not only volume
- Time asks for moments of delight

## Principles

- Incentives amplify advocacy; they rarely create it from nothing
- Simplicity beats complex multi-tier schemes for most products
- Referred users should land in a tailored experience
- Measure CAC and LTV of referred cohorts

## Verification

- [ ] Share flow is simple and mobile-friendly
- [ ] Incentives are clear and deliverable
- [ ] Fraud controls exist
- [ ] Referral quality is monitored
