# Copywriting

## Overview

Copywriting is salesmanship in words. Great copy clarifies the offer, reduces friction in the mind, and makes the next step obvious — without hype that destroys trust.

## When to Use

- Landing pages and product pages
- Ads and social posts
- Email subject lines and bodies
- CTAs, headlines, and value propositions
- Campaign narratives

## Core Practices

- Lead with the customer’s problem or desired outcome
- Make the offer and differentiator concrete
- Use specific proof over vague claims
- Write for scanning: short paragraphs, strong opens
- One primary CTA; remove competing asks
- Match tone to audience and channel

## Principles

- Clarity > cleverness
- Benefits rooted in real jobs-to-be-done
- Specificity builds belief
- Edit ruthlessly — every word should earn its place

## Verification

- [ ] A stranger understands the offer in seconds
- [ ] Proof supports the main claim
- [ ] The next action is unmistakable
