# Email Marketing

## Overview

Email remains one of the highest-ROI channels when it’s relevant, well-timed, and permission-based. Poor email trains people to ignore you.

## When to Use

- Lifecycle and onboarding sequences
- Newsletters and thought leadership
- Promotional campaigns and launches
- Re-engagement and win-back flows

## Core Practices

- Segment by behavior, lifecycle stage, and preference
- Write subject lines and preheaders that set honest expectations
- One primary CTA per email in most cases
- Design for mobile scanning
- Test cadence; protect deliverability (auth, list hygiene)
- Measure clicks, conversion, unsubscribes, and spam complaints

## Principles

- Permission and relevance beat list size
- Lifecycle email usually outperforms one-off blasts
- Every email should earn the next open
- Make unsubscribe easy — trapped subscribers hurt you
