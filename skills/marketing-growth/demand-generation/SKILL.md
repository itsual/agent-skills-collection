# Demand Generation

## Overview

Demand generation creates and captures interest from people who fit the ICP. It connects content, channels, and sales handoff into a measurable pipeline engine.

## When to Use

- Pipeline creation for sales-led or hybrid motions
- Integrated campaign planning
- Improving MQL/SQL quality and volume
- Coordinating marketing and SDRs/AEs

## Core Practices

- Define ICP and qualification criteria with sales
- Plan always-on engines + timed campaigns
- Align offers to awareness stage
- Orchestrate channels toward shared conversion goals
- Establish SLA for lead follow-up
- Report on pipeline and revenue, not only leads

## Principles

- Quality of demand > quantity of leads
- Hand-raisers and intent signals beat arbitrary lead scores alone
- Sales and marketing must share definitions of success
- Nurture is part of demand, not a consolation prize

## Verification

- [ ] ICP and stages are agreed with sales
- [ ] Campaigns have pipeline targets
- [ ] Follow-up SLAs exist and are monitored
