# Brand Strategy

## Overview

Brand strategy is the long-term system of meaning around a product or company. It shapes how people recognize, remember, and prefer you.

## When to Use

- Brand creation or rebrand
- Aligning multi-channel expression
- Differentiating in crowded markets
- Guiding agencies and internal creators

## Core Elements

- Purpose and promise
- Audience and brand positioning
- Personality and tone
- Visual and verbal identity principles
- Brand architecture (if multiple products)
- Guardrails for expression

## Principles

- Distinctiveness enables memory
- Consistency builds familiarity; flexibility allows relevance
- Brand is what people experience, not only what you claim
- Strategy should make creative decisions easier, not harder

## Verification

- [ ] Teams can apply brand principles without constant escalation
- [ ] Expression feels coherent across channels
- [ ] Brand choices support positioning, not fight it
