# Partnership Marketing

## Overview

Partnership marketing multiplies reach by aligning with companies that share customers but aren’t direct competitors. Success depends on mutual value and clear execution ownership.

## When to Use

- Integration and ecosystem co-marketing
- Co-webinars, content, and bundled offers
- Affiliate and channel partner programs
- Strategic brand collaborations

## Core Practices

- Select partners for audience overlap and brand fit
- Define joint value proposition and offers
- Agree on goals, metrics, and split of work
- Create shared assets and landing experiences
- Enable partner sales/marketing teams
- Review performance and renew intentionally

## Principles

- Mutual benefit or it dies
- Clarity of ownership prevents stalled campaigns
- Start with one sharp motion before expanding scope
- Measure pipeline and revenue, not only impressions

## Verification

- [ ] Partner fit and goals are documented
- [ ] Joint offer is clear to the shared audience
- [ ] Success metrics and owners exist on both sides
