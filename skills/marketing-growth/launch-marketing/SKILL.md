# Launch Marketing

## Overview

Launch marketing turns a product moment into market attention and adoption. It aligns messaging, channels, enablement, and measurement around a clear launch goal.

## When to Use

- New product or major feature launches
- Rebrands or major packaging changes
- Market entry campaigns
- Timed moments (events, seasons, partnerships)

## Core Practices

- Define launch tier and success metrics
- Craft narrative and proof points
- Coordinate owned, earned, and paid channels
- Enable sales, CS, and support before go-live
- Sequence teaser → launch → sustain
- Run a post-launch review

## Principles

- Launch is a process, not a day
- Internal readiness matters as much as external noise
- Match ambition of tactics to strategic importance
- Capture learnings for the next launch

## Verification

- [ ] Launch goal and metrics are explicit
- [ ] GTM teams are enabled before external push
- [ ] Channels and narrative are coordinated
