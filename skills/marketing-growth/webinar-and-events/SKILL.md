# Webinar and Events

## Overview

Events create focused attention. They work when the topic is sharp, promotion is targeted, the experience is well-run, and follow-up is systematic.

## When to Use

- Educational webinars and demos
- Product launches and virtual summits
- Field events and dinners
- Community and user conferences

## Core Practices

- Define the audience and outcome before the format
- Promote with message-market fit and enough lead time
- Design the agenda for engagement, not monologue
- Prep speakers and tech thoroughly
- Capture questions and intent signals
- Follow up fast with segmented next steps

## Principles

- Value-first content earns the right to a soft CTA
- No-shows are normal — plan recording and nurture
- Post-event pipeline work is part of the event
- Measure registrants, attendees, engaged attendees, and downstream conversion

## Verification

- [ ] Target audience and offer are clear in promotion
- [ ] Run-of-show and tech are rehearsed
- [ ] Follow-up sequences are ready before the event ends
