# Goal Setting and OKRs (HR)

## Overview

Goal systems focus energy. HR’s role is enabling clarity, alignment, and cadence — not owning business strategy content.

## When to Use

- Company and team goal cycles
- OKR design and coaching
- Aligning individual goals to team outcomes
- Connecting goals to reviews without bureaucratic overload

## Core Practices

- Ensure goals are outcome-oriented where possible
- Limit goal count to maintain focus
- Create check-in cadence, not set-and-forget
- Align upward and across teams on dependencies
- Teach managers to write and coach good goals
- Avoid turning every goal into a performance weapon

## Principles

- Too many goals = no priorities
- Activity metrics disguised as OKRs create busywork
- Learning and stretch goals need psychological safety
- HR facilitates process quality; leaders own substance

## Verification

- [ ] Goal cycles have clear timelines and owners
- [ ] Quality examples and coaching are available
- [ ] Goals are visible and reviewed mid-cycle
