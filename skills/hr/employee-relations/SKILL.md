# Employee Relations

## Overview

Employee relations maintains a fair and lawful workplace when issues arise between people, or between people and the organization. Consistency and documentation protect everyone.

## When to Use

- Conflict and grievance handling
- Policy violation responses
- Climate issues on teams
- Advising managers on difficult people situations

## Core Practices

- Intake issues with neutrality and confidentiality appropriate to the case
- Assess severity and choose proportionate paths
- Investigate with fairness when facts are disputed
- Apply policies consistently across similar cases
- Document decisions and rationale
- Address systemic causes, not only individual incidents

## Principles

- Speed and fairness must coexist
- Inconsistent treatment creates legal and cultural risk
- Managers need coaching, not only escalation dumping
- Prevention through culture and clarity beats pure reaction

## Verification

- [ ] Intake and escalation paths are clear
- [ ] Similar cases yield consistent approaches
- [ ] Themes are reviewed for systemic fixes
