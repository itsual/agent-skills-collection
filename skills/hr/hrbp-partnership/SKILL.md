# HRBP Partnership

## Overview

HR Business Partners connect business strategy to people strategy within a client group. Effective HRBPs diagnose, advise, and build leader capability — while still ensuring reliable HR operations.

## When to Use

- Defining HRBP operating models
- Coaching leaders on org and talent decisions
- Prioritizing people initiatives for a business unit
- Balancing strategic work with employee relations reality

## Core Practices

- Know the business model, metrics, and pressures deeply
- Bring data and options, not only policy answers
- Build trust through confidentiality and follow-through
- Develop managers as the multiplier
- Sequence people initiatives to leader capacity
- Escalate systemic issues that local fixes cannot solve

## Principles

- Order-taking is not partnering
- Credibility comes from business fluency + people expertise
- HRBPs should not become a parallel management layer
- Centers of excellence and ops must support the partner model

## Verification

- [ ] HRBPs have clear client groups and priorities
- [ ] Leaders seek proactive talent counsel
- [ ] Strategic vs transactional time is consciously managed
