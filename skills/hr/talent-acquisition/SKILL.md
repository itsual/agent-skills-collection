# Talent Acquisition

## Overview

Talent acquisition is the end-to-end system for finding and hiring people who can succeed in the role and culture. Quality of hire and time-to-fill both matter.

## When to Use

- Building or redesigning recruiting processes
- Improving conversion across funnel stages
- Employer branding for hiring
- Scaling hiring for growth or new functions

## Core Practices

- Define role success profiles before sourcing
- Build diverse, proactive pipelines — not only inbound
- Structure selection stages with clear pass criteria
- Reduce bias through process design, not only training slogans
- Partner with hiring managers on ownership of outcomes
- Measure quality of hire, not only speed and cost

## Principles

- A fast bad hire is expensive
- Candidate experience is employer brand in action
- Hiring manager capability is part of TA system design
- Data should improve funnel decisions continuously

## Verification

- [ ] Role requirements are defined before active search
- [ ] Funnel metrics are tracked stage by stage
- [ ] Quality-of-hire signals exist post-start
