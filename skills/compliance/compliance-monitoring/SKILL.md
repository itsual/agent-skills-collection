# Compliance Monitoring

## Overview

Monitoring tests whether compliance controls operate as intended. It is the feedback loop that turns policies into assurance.

## When to Use

- Annual compliance monitoring/testing plans
- Continuous monitoring of high-risk processes
- Post-incident control validation
- Exam readiness through proactive issue discovery

## Core Practices

- Risk-base the monitoring plan (what, how often, sample size)
- Define test procedures and pass/fail criteria
- Use independent testers where required
- Document workpapers sufficient for review
- Escalate issues with severity and remediation tracking
- Feed results into risk assessment and training

## Principles

- Monitoring without remediation is performance art
- Sampling should match risk and population
- Repeat findings indicate systemic failure
- Automation can expand coverage for rule-based checks

## Verification

- [ ] Monitoring plan maps to high compliance risks
- [ ] Issues are logged with owners and due dates
- [ ] Results are reported to appropriate governance
