# Board Pack

## Overview

Board packs should enable effective oversight and decision-making. They prioritize material information, clear recommendations, and easy navigation.

## When to Use

- Board of directors meeting packs
- Governance or committee papers
- Investor or advisory board updates at board level
- High-stakes leadership decision packs

## Typical Contents

1. Agenda and meeting objectives
2. CEO / leadership summary
3. Key performance and financial highlights
4. Strategic items requiring decision or discussion
5. Risk and compliance update
6. Consent agenda items
7. Appendices / supporting detail

## Principles

- Lead with what requires attention or decision
- Keep the main pack short; put detail in appendices
- Use consistent structure and status indicators across meetings
- Make recommendations and options explicit
- Flag changes since the last meeting
- Ensure accuracy — board materials carry high trust expectations

## Verification

- [ ] Directors can identify decisions required within minutes
- [ ] Material risks and performance issues are visible
- [ ] Supporting detail is available without cluttering the main narrative
