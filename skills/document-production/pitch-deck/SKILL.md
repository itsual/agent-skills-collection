# Pitch Deck

## Overview

A pitch deck is a visual argument. Every slide should advance the case for why the audience should care, believe, and act.

## When to Use

- Investor / fundraising pitches
- Sales or solution pitches
- Partnership or alliance proposals
- Internal executive pitches for major initiatives

## Classic Narrative Arc

1. Problem / opportunity
2. Why now
3. Solution
4. Product / how it works
5. Market and customer
6. Traction / evidence
7. Business model
8. Competition / differentiation
9. Team
10. The ask and use of funds / next steps

## Principles

- One idea per slide
- Lead with insight, not features
- Make the “why you” unmistakable
- Use evidence over adjectives
- Design for both presentation and leave-behind reading
- Keep the core deck short; put detail in appendix

## Verification

- [ ] A listener can restate the core opportunity and ask after one pass
- [ ] Each slide has a clear job in the argument
- [ ] Claims are supported by credible evidence
