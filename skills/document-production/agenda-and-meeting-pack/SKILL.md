# Agenda and Meeting Pack

## Overview

Good agendas and meeting packs turn meetings from status dumps into decision forums. They set purpose, timeboxes, and preparation expectations.

## When to Use

- Team or project meetings
- Steering committees and governance forums
- Workshops and decision sessions
- Client or stakeholder meetings that need structure

## Agenda Essentials

- Meeting purpose / desired outcomes
- Date, time, location/link, attendees
- Timed agenda items with owners
- Pre-read requirements
- Decision or input needed for each major item

## Meeting Pack Principles

- Distribute pre-reads early enough to be read
- Put decision items ahead of pure information items when possible
- Keep supporting documents short and clearly labeled
- Include a clear “what we need from you” for participants
- Leave room for discussion, not just presentation

## Verification

- [ ] Purpose of the meeting is explicit
- [ ] Participants know what to prepare
- [ ] Time allocation matches importance of topics
- [ ] Decision items are clearly marked
