# Training Material

## Overview

Effective training materials are built around learning outcomes, not just information transfer. They combine explanation, examples, and practice.

## When to Use

- Internal enablement and onboarding training
- Workshops and instructor-led sessions
- Self-paced learning guides
- Sales or partner enablement content

## Design Principles

- Start from clear learning objectives
- Chunk content into manageable sections
- Mix explanation with examples and exercises
- Include checks for understanding
- Provide job aids or reference sheets for later use
- Design for the delivery format (live, remote, self-paced)

## Useful Components

- Learning objectives
- Agenda / flow
- Core concept explanations
- Worked examples
- Practice exercises or scenarios
- Summary and next steps
- Reference / cheat sheet

## Verification

- [ ] Learners can state what they will be able to do after the training
- [ ] Practice opportunities exist for key skills
- [ ] Materials work in the intended delivery setting
