# Sales Playbooks

## Overview

A sales playbook captures the repeatable path to win in a defined situation — who to target, what to say, which proof to use, and how deals typically progress.

## When to Use

- Scaling a motion that works for top reps
- Launching into a new segment or vertical
- Competitive displacement campaigns
- Onboarding and ongoing coaching

## Core Contents

- Ideal customer profile for the play
- Triggers and entry points
- Discovery questions and talk tracks
- Demo path and proof points
- Common objections and responses
- Stage exit criteria and mutual action plans
- Competitive notes when relevant

## Principles

- Playbooks should be short enough to use live
- Built from real wins, not theoretical process
- Update from field learning continuously
- One playbook per distinct motion beats a giant manual

## Verification

- [ ] Play is specific to a segment or situation
- [ ] Reps can apply it without heavy interpretation
- [ ] Results of the play are tracked
