# Discovery Calls

## Overview

Discovery is where deals are won or lost early. Strong discovery diagnoses the problem, quantifies impact, maps stakeholders, and tests mutual fit before a demo or proposal.

## When to Use

- First meaningful sales conversations
- Qualification after inbound interest
- Multi-threading into complex accounts
- Coaching reps on call quality

## Core Practices

- Prepare with research on company, role, and likely pains
- Open with context and permission to ask questions
- Explore current state, desired state, and obstacles
- Quantify impact (time, money, risk, opportunity)
- Identify decision process, timeline, and competitors
- Summarize and confirm mutual next steps

## Principles

- Diagnose before you prescribe
- Curiosity beats scripted interrogation
- Silence and follow-ups reveal more than rapid pitching
- Disqualify early when fit is poor — protect time

## Verification

- [ ] Pain, impact, and stakeholders are documented
- [ ] Next steps are mutual and specific
- [ ] Fit (or non-fit) is clear enough to advance or exit
