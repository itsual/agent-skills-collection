# Win-Loss Analysis

## Overview

Win-loss analysis replaces anecdotes with structured learning. It reveals patterns in why deals are won or lost across product, price, process, competition, and relationships.

## When to Use

- Win rate is declining or unexplained
- Entering competitive markets
- Feeding product roadmap and positioning
- Coaching based on real buyer feedback

## Core Practices

- Interview buyers (won and lost) with neutral framing
- Capture internal deal team perspectives separately
- Code reasons into consistent categories
- Look for patterns by segment, competitor, and deal size
- Share findings with product, marketing, and sales leadership
- Turn insights into specific play and messaging changes

## Principles

- Buyer truth > internal storytelling
- Losses teach as much as wins — sometimes more
- Avoid blaming individuals; seek systemic patterns
- Close the loop: analysis without action is theater

## Verification

- [ ] Both wins and losses are sampled
- [ ] Buyer voice is included, not only CRM notes
- [ ] Insights produce concrete changes in GTM or product
