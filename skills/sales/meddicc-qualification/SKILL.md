# MEDDICC Qualification

## Overview

MEDDICC is a qualification framework for complex B2B sales. It forces clarity on pain, value, power, process, and competition — reducing surprise losses late in the cycle.

## When to Use

- Enterprise and mid-market multi-threaded deals
- Pipeline reviews and deal inspection
- Coaching reps on deal rigor
- Improving forecast reliability

## Elements

- **Metrics** — quantified economic impact
- **Economic Buyer** — who owns budget and final yes
- **Decision Criteria** — formal and informal evaluation factors
- **Decision Process** — steps, timeline, and approvers
- **Identify Pain** — compelling reason to act
- **Champion** — internal seller with power and influence
- **Competition** — alternatives including status quo

## Principles

- Missing elements are risks, not paperwork
- Champions must have influence, not only enthusiasm
- Metrics turn features into business cases
- Revisit MEDDICC as deals evolve

## Verification

- [ ] Each element has evidence, not assumptions
- [ ] Gaps have explicit next-step plans
- [ ] Forecast confidence reflects MEDDICC completeness
