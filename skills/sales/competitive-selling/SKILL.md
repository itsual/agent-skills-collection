# Competitive Selling

## Overview

Competitive selling is about shaping decision criteria toward your strengths and the competitor’s gaps — without badmouthing that damages credibility.

## When to Use

- Head-to-head evaluations
- Incumbent displacement
- Late-stage competitive threats
- Building competitive battle cards for the field

## Core Practices

- Learn the competitor’s likely narrative and landmines
- Align discovery to criteria where you win
- Use third-party proof and customer stories
- Help champions sell internally against alternatives
- Address status quo as a competitor, not only vendors
- Stay factual; let the buyer draw conclusions

## Principles

- Never trash-talk; it signals insecurity
- Win on criteria that matter to this buyer, not generic feature lists
- Incumbent inertia is often the hardest competitor
- Post-win, capture what actually mattered for future plays

## Verification

- [ ] Decision criteria favor differentiated strengths
- [ ] Champion can articulate why you vs alternatives
- [ ] Competitive risks are logged and mitigated
