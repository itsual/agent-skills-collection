# Social Selling

## Overview

Social selling uses professional networks to research, engage, and start conversations. It works when value and relevance come first — not connection-request spam.

## When to Use

- Outbound prospecting via social
- Building warm paths into accounts
- Establishing seller credibility in a niche
- Engaging buyers who are active on professional networks

## Core Practices

- Optimize profile for buyer-relevant clarity
- Engage with prospects’ content before pitching
- Share insights that demonstrate expertise
- Personalize connection and InMail with a real reason
- Integrate social touches into multi-channel sequences
- Track conversations and meetings, not only connection counts

## Principles

- Helpfulness precedes asks
- Automation that feels fake destroys reputation
- Social is one channel, not a full sales strategy
- Compliance with platform rules matters

## Verification

- [ ] Outreach is personalized and relevant
- [ ] Profile supports credibility for the ICP
- [ ] Social activity ties to pipeline outcomes
