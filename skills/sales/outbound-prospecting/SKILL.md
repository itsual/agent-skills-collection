# Outbound Prospecting

## Overview

Outbound creates pipeline by initiating conversations with people who fit the ICP but haven’t raised their hand yet. Relevance and persistence beat volume without targeting.

## When to Use

- Building pipeline in new segments or territories
- Supplementing inbound demand
- Account-based outreach into target accounts
- Coaching SDRs/AEs on prospecting craft

## Core Practices

- Define ICP and trigger events tightly
- Research enough to personalize meaningfully
- Write concise outreach focused on their problem, not your product
- Multi-channel sequences with thoughtful cadence
- Track replies, meetings, and quality — not only sends
- Iterate messaging based on response patterns

## Principles

- Personalization must be real, not mail-merge theater
- Respect attention; earn the reply
- Follow-up is where most meetings are won
- Compliance with email and calling rules is mandatory

## Verification

- [ ] Targeting criteria are clear and enforced
- [ ] Messaging speaks to buyer problems
- [ ] Metrics include meeting quality and conversion, not only activity
