# CRM Hygiene

## Overview

CRM is only as valuable as its data. Hygiene — accurate stages, contacts, next steps, and close dates — is the foundation of forecasting, coaching, and marketing attribution.

## When to Use

- Forecast accuracy problems
- Onboarding reps to CRM standards
- Preparing board or leadership reporting
- Enabling automation and sequencing tools

## Core Practices

- Define required fields by stage with clear meaning
- Enforce next-step and close-date discipline
- Close or recycle stale opportunities
- Keep primary contacts and buying roles updated
- Audit regularly; don’t wait for quarterly panic
- Make hygiene easy with defaults and lightweight process

## Principles

- Garbage in, garbage forecast
- Process should serve selling, not bureaucracy — but minimum standards are non-negotiable
- Managers model the hygiene they expect
- Clean data enables AI and automation later

## Verification

- [ ] Stage definitions are understood and applied
- [ ] Stale deals are reviewed on a cadence
- [ ] Leadership trusts CRM-sourced reports
