# Renewal and Upsell

## Overview

Renewals are not automatic. Upsells are not favors. Both rest on value delivered, relationship health, and timely commercial process.

## When to Use

- Upcoming renewals
- Expansion and cross-sell opportunities
- Price increase or package change conversations
- At-risk accounts approaching renewal

## Core Practices

- Start renewal prep well before the date
- Review adoption, outcomes, and open issues early
- Multi-thread so renewal isn’t hostage to one contact
- Tie upsell to new use cases or proven ROI
- Handle price changes with value narrative and options
- Coordinate CS, account management, and product on risk

## Principles

- Surprise renewals with problems are self-inflicted
- Expansion follows success; force-fit upsells damage trust
- Document value throughout the year, not only at renewal
- Procurement will still negotiate — prepare

## Verification

- [ ] Renewal risk is assessed early enough to act
- [ ] Value evidence is ready before commercial talks
- [ ] Upsell thesis matches customer priorities
