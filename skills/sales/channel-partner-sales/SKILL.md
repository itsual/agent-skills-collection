# Channel Partner Sales

## Overview

Channel sales multiplies reach through partners who already have customer relationships. It requires different enablement, deal hygiene, and conflict rules than direct sales.

## When to Use

- Reseller and referral partner programs
- Co-sell with technology or consulting partners
- Geographic or vertical coverage via partners
- Deal registration and conflict resolution

## Core Practices

- Define partner types and expected motions clearly
- Enable partners with simple plays and collateral
- Implement fair deal registration rules
- Align incentives so partners prioritize your offer
- Support co-sell deals with joint account planning
- Measure partner-sourced and partner-influenced revenue separately

## Principles

- Partners succeed when the motion is easy and profitable for them
- Channel conflict kills partner trust quickly
- Direct and channel rules must be explicit
- Quality of partner engagement beats partner count

## Verification

- [ ] Partner motion and rules are documented
- [ ] Enablement is usable by partner sellers
- [ ] Registration and attribution are trusted
